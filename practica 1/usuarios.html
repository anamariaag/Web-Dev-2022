TJ DeFalco

Born: 1997

Team: Asseco Resovia, USA

He is part of the US national team, a participant at the Olympic Games Tokyo 2020. At the professional club level, he plays for Asseco Resovia. Parents are Torey and Gina. T.J. was one of six siblings who all played volleyball and all have names starting with T... sister Teagan represented U.S. on 2019 Beach U21 National Team and played beach for Grand Canyon University. At 24, DeFalco and teammate Kyle Ensing were the youngest players on the U.S. team for the 2020 Tokyo Olympic Games. TJ competed in beach volleyball at the 2014 Youth Olympic Games and at the U19 World Championships. Played junior volleyball at Huntington Beach Club.






Logan Tom

Born: 1981

Team: retired, USA

Logan Maile Lei Tom is an American indoor volleyball, beach volleyball player, and current head coach of the Israel women's national volleyball team. She is a four-time Olympian at the outside hitter position. At age 19, Logan became the youngest woman ever to be selected for an American Olympic volleyball team when she competed at the 2000 Olympic Games in Sydney.






Ivan Zaytsev

Born: 1988

Team: Lube, Italy

Nicknamed lo Zar, is an Italian volleyball player of Russian origin, the captain of Italy men's national volleyball team, a bronze medalist of the Olympic Games London 2012, silver medalist of the European Championship, bronze medalist of the World League, Italian Champion and silver medalist of the Olympic Games Rio 2016.
